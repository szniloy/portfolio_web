About Me

Engineering mobile experiences that users remember and businesses rely on.

I'm Md Sahiduzzaman Niloy , a Kotlin & Java Mobile Developer, Kotlin Multiplatform (KMP) Expert, and UI/UX Designer , passionate about building premium Android, iOS, and cross-platform applications. From startup MVPs to full-scale business solutions, I transform ideas into fast, scalable, and production-ready mobile products.

I believe great apps aren't just beautifully designed, they're engineered for performance, maintainability, and long-term growth. Using Kotlin, Jetpack Compose, KMP, Firebase, REST APIs, and Clean Architecture, I build applications that are reliable, intuitive, and ready for real users.

When you work with me, you get more than code, you get clear communication, attention to detail, and a developer who genuinely cares about your product's success from the first idea to launch.
